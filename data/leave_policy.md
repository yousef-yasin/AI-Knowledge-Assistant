# Employee Leave Policy

## Annual Leave

- Full-time employees receive **21 paid annual leave days** every year.
- Annual leave requests must be submitted at least **5 working days** before the leave date.
- Approval from the employee's direct manager is required.
- Employees may carry forward a maximum of **5 unused annual leave days** to the following year.

---

## Sick Leave

- Employees receive **14 paid sick leave days** per year.
- A medical certificate is required if sick leave exceeds **2 consecutive working days**.

---

## Maternity Leave

Eligible employees receive **70 calendar days** of fully paid maternity leave.

---

## Paternity Leave

Eligible employees receive **3 paid working days** of paternity leave.

---

## Emergency Leave

Employees may receive up to **3 paid emergency leave days** per year with manager approval.

---

## Unpaid Leave

Employees may request unpaid leave for a maximum of **30 calendar days**.
Approval from both the department manager and Human Resources is required.

---

## Public Holidays

Employees are entitled to all official public holidays announced by the Government of Jordan.

---

## Leave Cancellation

Approved leave may be cancelled only after agreement between the employee and the direct manager.

---

## Leave Balance

Employees can check their annual leave balance through the Human Resources department.
